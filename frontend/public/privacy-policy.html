Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

1. Introduction

Mulema ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how your personal information is collected, used, and disclosed by Mulema when you use our mobile application.

2. Information We Collect

To provide you with the best experience on Mulema, we collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: Name, email address, and username provided during sign-up or via social login (Google, Facebook, Apple).
  • Learning & Progress Data: Language learning progress, streak data, exercises completed, and time spent on the platform.
  • R2 User Assets: Profile pictures (avatars) uploaded by the user, stored securely.
  • Technical Data: Device type, operating system version, and anonymous usage statistics to improve app performance.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information strictly to:

  • Create and maintain your user account.
  • Track and visualize your language learning progress and streaks.
  • Personalize your cultural learning experience.
  • Notify you about important account updates and security alerts.

4. Data Sharing

We do not sell, trade, or rent your personal data to third parties. Data is only shared with trusted infrastructure providers (like Cloudflare for storage and AWS/Vercel for hosting) necessary to run the service.

5. Data Security

We implement industry-standard encryption and security measures to protect your data. All user assets are stored in secure buckets, and authentication is handled via industry-standard protocols.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to access, correct, export, or delete your personal information at any time.

7. Data Deletion

In-App Deletion (Recommended): You can permanently delete your account and all associated data (progress, statistics, and profile pictures) directly within the app:
Settings → Supprimer le compte → Type "DELETE" to confirm.

Manual Request: Alternatively, you may contact us at mulemasupport@gmail.com to request account removal. We will process your request within 30 days.

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at: mulemasupport@gmail.com
