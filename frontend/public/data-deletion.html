Data Deletion Instructions

How to Delete Your Data

You can request deletion of your Mulema account and personal data at any time.

Option 1: Delete from the App

Go to your Profile settings in the Mulema app and select "Delete Account".

Option 2: Contact Us

Email: mulemasupport@gmail.com

Subject: Data Deletion Request

Please include your registered email address in the request.

What Happens When You Request Deletion

  • Your account will be permanently deleted
  • All your learning progress will be removed
  • All personal information will be erased
  • This process is irreversible

Processing Time: We will process your data deletion request within 30 days.
