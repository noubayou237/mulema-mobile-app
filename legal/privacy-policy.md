# Privacy Policy for Mulema

Last Updated: May 18, 2026

At Mulema, we are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and share information about you when you use our mobile application (the "App") and our services.

## 1. Information We Collect

### A. Information You Provide to Us
- **Account Information:** When you sign up, we collect your email address, username, and password. You may also provide a profile picture (avatar).
- **Usage Data:** We track your progress, XP, cowries (hearts), and language preferences to provide you with a personalized learning experience.

### B. Information Collected Automatically
- **Device Information:** We may collect information about the device you use to access the App, including the hardware model and operating system.
- **Log Information:** We record certain log file information, such as your IP address and app usage patterns.

## 2. How We Use Your Information
We use the information we collect to:
- Provide, maintain, and improve our services.
- Personalize your learning experience.
- Respond to your comments and questions.
- Send you technical notices and security alerts.
- Monitor and analyze trends, usage, and activities in connection with our App.

## 3. Sharing of Information
We do **not** sell your personal data. We may share information as follows:
- **With Other Users:** Your username, avatar, and total XP are visible to other users on the leaderboard/community screen.
- **Service Providers:** We may share information with vendors who perform services on our behalf (e.g., database hosting, email delivery).
- **Legal Requirements:** We may disclose information if required to do so by law.

## 4. Your Choices
- **Account Information:** You may update or correct your account information at any time by logging into the App and visiting your settings.
- **Data Deletion:** You may request the deletion of your account and all associated data by contacting us at support@mulema.app or through the in-app settings.

## 5. User-Generated Content (UGC)
Mulema includes community features such as leaderboards. Users are expected to maintain appropriate usernames. We provide reporting tools for users to flag inappropriate content. We reserve the right to remove or block users who violate our community guidelines.

## 6. Data Security
We take reasonable measures to help protect information about you from loss, theft, misuse, and unauthorized access.

## 7. Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
Email: support@mulema.app
Website: https://mulema.app
